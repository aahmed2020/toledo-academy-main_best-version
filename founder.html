Founder

Meet the academic and industry leader behind Toledo Academy

Dr. Abdulaziz (Aziz) Ahmed

Founder, Toledo Academy

Aziz is a researcher, educator, and consultant with more than a decade of experience in artificial intelligence, health informatics, and operations analytics. He founded Toledo Academy to bridge the gap between academic AI and practical, measurable outcomes for small and mid-size organizations.

AI Decision Support Machine Learning Health Informatics Operations Analytics

  • Principal Investigator, AHRQ R21/R33 predictive decision support grant

Focus Areas

  • Custom AI assistants with retrieval and guardrails
  • Executive dashboards with forecasting and alerts
